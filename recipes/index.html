Kate's Simple Recipe Collection

This is a collection of recipes that I have found on the internet and tried at home. I love cooking and have found it really useful to have a mobile copy of the recipes that I am making, but too often, the sites/blogs that have the recipes are bogged down by ads and other extras that I just don't need. I have created this page to enable better organization of recipes I love in order to read them on my phone while cooking and sharing them with my friends and family!

~Enjoy, Kate~
